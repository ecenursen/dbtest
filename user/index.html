User Guide¶

Welcome to *HastayimDB* user guide. Here, there will be information from A to Z on how to completely use our application.

Login

Navigate to Login Page by clicking the Login button on the top right corner of your browser.

Login Page

You should use your Citizenship Number (TCKN) and Password that is provided to you. After this step, new functionalities of our application will be provided to you depending on your authorization.
